Omelete Recipe

Omelete is made of eggs, and is one of the famous breakfasts, eaten all around the world. Every country has its own way of cooking omelete, and in this article I will explain how we cook omelete here in Afghanistan

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Pour oil in the pan
  2. Heat the oil for 1 min, until its stops cracking noise
  3. Put the tomatoes in the pan, and cook them
  4. Put salt on the tomatoes
  5. Put the eggs on top of the tomatoes
  6. Put salt and spices on eggs, and cover it and let it cook until the egg yoke is covered with whites
