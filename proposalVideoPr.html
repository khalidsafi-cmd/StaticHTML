Proposal Writing

Writing proposals is an important task for lots of major businesses. Governments and big companies all around the world do not want to micro manage everything, and thus want to give these tasks as contracts to other smaller businesses to fullfill, for which they share a document called Request for Proposals, and then smaller businesses who want to win the contract write their proposals which consists of the following parts

Introduction/Problem Statement

In this stage the business introduces themselves, what their relevant experiences are, and what they have achieved as a business. They acknowledge the requirements of the job.

Proposed solution

After the acnknowledgement of the problem, the business proposes the solutions to that problem, and they show how they can solve the problem, by showing their experience in similar cases, and their expertiese in this matter.

Conclusions/Benifits

In this section the business concludes on the proposal, and reaffirm their ability for the job, and state the benifits of them doing the job.
